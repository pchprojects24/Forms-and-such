B. Braun Infusomat® Space

Type: Volumetric infusion pump (Space system).

Quick Start

  1. Power: Connect to AC power or confirm adequate battery, then power on.
  2. Pre-checks: Confirm correct profile/unit configuration for your site and verify self-test completes.
  3. Set/tubing: Use the correct Infusomat Space pump set. Prime per local policy.
  4. Load line: Open door, route tubing exactly as shown on the pump’s routing guides, ensure clamps/sensors are correctly engaged, then close/lock door.
  5. Program: Select therapy/mode as configured locally. Enter required parameters (commonly 2 of 3: rate/VTBI/time).
  6. Connect & start: Trace line to patient, confirm correct access, then press Start.
  7. Monitor: Verify flow, watch for alarms (occlusion/air/door/line issues), respond per on-screen message and local policy.
  8. Stop/end: Use stop/standby as trained; clamp as required; unload line per procedure.

Common pitfalls

  • Incorrect tubing type (not an approved Infusomat Space set).
  • Misrouted tubing through guides/sensors → door/line alarms or inaccurate delivery.
  • Closed clamp/kink upstream → upstream occlusion alarms.
  • Not confirming correct profile/drug library configuration for your unit.

Do’s and Don’ts

  • Do trace the line end-to-end before starting.
  • Do verify parameters on the run screen after pressing Start.
  • Don’t bypass routing guides or force the door closed.
  • Don’t use unapproved tubing/sets.
